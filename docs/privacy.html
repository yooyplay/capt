Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1

Your videos never leave your device

Capt does not upload, transmit, or store your video files. The Mac app processes everything locally using Whisper AI and ffmpeg on your machine. The browser tool transcribes and burns captions entirely within your browser using WebAssembly. No file ever reaches a server.

2

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as page views, session duration, and general location (country/city). This helps us understand how people use the site. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

3

Google AdSense & Cookies

This website may display Google AdSense advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to show you ads based on your interests and prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ad Settings or via aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data, see How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites.

4

No accounts, no personal data

Capt has no signup, no login, and collects no personal information such as names, email addresses, or payment details. We have no user database.

5

Third-party links

This site links to GitHub and Ko-fi. Those services have their own privacy policies which we do not control.
